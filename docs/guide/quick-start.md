# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with PaperWeight in just a few minutes!

## Step 1: Sign Up & Create Account

1. Visit the PaperWeight app
2. Click **Sign Up** to create your account
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

## Step 2: Write Your First Workout

### Prepare Your Paper Log

You can use any format you prefer! PaperWeight's AI is flexible and can understand various handwriting styles and formats. Common formats include:

```
Date: [Today's Date]

Squats:
Set 1: 135 lbs x 8 reps
Set 2: 155 lbs x 6 reps
Set 3: 175 lbs x 4 reps

Bench Press:
Set 1: 145 lbs x 8 reps
Set 2: 155 lbs x 6 reps
Set 3: 165 lbs x 4 reps

Deadlifts:
1 x 225 lbs x 5 reps
```

### During Your Workout

1. **Focus on your workout** - no phone needed!
2. Write down each exercise as you complete it
3. Include sets, reps, weights, and any notes
4. Don't worry about perfect formatting - our AI is smart

## Step 3: Upload Your Workout

### Take a Clear Photo

After your workout:

1. Open the PaperWeight app
2. Navigate to **Upload** (ğŸ“· icon)
3. Take a clear, well-lit photo of your workout log
4. Make sure all text is visible and legible

### Tips for Best Results

- **Good lighting**: Natural light works best
- **Steady hands**: Avoid blur and shadows  
- **Full page**: Include the entire workout in the frame
- **Flat surface**: Lay the paper on a flat surface
- **Dark ink**: Blue or black ink works better than pencil

## Step 4: Review & Confirm

### AI Processing

1. Upload your photo
2. Wait for AI to process
3. Review the extracted workout data
4. Make any necessary corrections

### What Gets Extracted

PaperWeight automatically identifies:

- **Exercise names** (and maps them to our database)
- **Sets and reps**
- **Weights and resistance**
- **Workout date and duration**
- **Additional notes**

## Your First Week

### Best Practices

- **Start simple**: Use basic exercises you're familiar with
- **Be consistent**: Upload workouts regularly
- **Review data**: Check your stats page weekly
- **Adjust format**: Find a handwriting style that works best with the AI

### Common Questions

**Q: What if the AI misreads my handwriting?**
A: You can edit any workout data after upload.

**Q: Can I upload old workout logs?**
A: Yes! Upload photos of previous workouts to build your historical data.

**Q: Do I need to use a specific format?**
A: No, but consistent formatting helps the AI perform better.
